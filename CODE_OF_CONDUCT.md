# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) guidelines:

1. Be respectful and inclusive. Harassment of any kind is not tolerated.
2. Assume good intent and provide constructive feedback.
3. Use welcoming and inclusive language.
4. Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainers at `security@english-ia.local`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
